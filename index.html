Essential Care Guides

Java Moss

Extremely hardy, low light tolerant, perfect for beginners and aquariums. Grows fast on almost any surface.

Closed Terrariums

Self-sustaining ecosystems. Water very rarely (every 3-6 months). Great for low-maintenance setups.

Sphagnum Moss

Excellent moisture retention. Use as living substrate or top dressing. Loves bright indirect light.
